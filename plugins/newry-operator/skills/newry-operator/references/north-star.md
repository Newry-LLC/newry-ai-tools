# North Star — Newry AI Program

**Version:** v2.1 — 2026-05-19

## North Star Statement

**The AI-native boutique. Exceptional for clients. Exceptionally profitable for us.**

---

## Business Outcome Target

**2x profit per consultant by May 2027 vs. FY2025 baseline.**

This is the number the program is ultimately accountable to. Everything else — adoption, quality scores, tool coverage — is a leading indicator toward it.

---

## Leading Indicators

**1. Active weekly usage rate**
% of Newry consultants with ≥1 tool run in the trailing 7 days (30-day rolling average).
Measures adoption.

**2. Eval pass rate**
% of skill runs that pass quality review against defined eval criteria.
Measures quality.

---

## Milestones

| Date | Target | What it signals |
|---|---|---|
| July 10, 2026 | 50% active weekly usage rate | Handoff-ready |
| Oct 2026 | 80% active weekly usage rate | Firm-wide adoption |
| May 2027 | 2x profit per consultant vs. FY2025 | Business outcomes realized |

---

## Why "same team, materially better work, the P&L shows it"

**"Same team"** — not a headcount story. Makes existing people more capable.

**"Materially better work"** — better is the point, not just faster. Quality is non-negotiable.

**"The P&L shows it"** — accountable to business outcomes, not just usage. If adoption is high but margins don't move, something is wrong.
